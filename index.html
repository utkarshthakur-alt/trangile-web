Industries We Serve

Infor WMS capabilities shaped around the operating realities of each warehouse network.

Retail & Omnichannel Fulfillment

Trangile helps retailers use Infor WMS to balance store replenishment, ecommerce fulfillment, returns, and inventory availability across fast-moving warehouse networks.

  • Unified B2B, B2C, store, and ecommerce execution
  • Piece-level scanning, pack logic, and returns control
  • POS, OMS, ERP, and transport integration

3PL & Logistics

For contract logistics and 3PL operators, Trangile configures Infor WMS to manage multiple customers, activity-based billing, SLA commitments, and value-added service flows.

  • Multi-client rules, ownership, billing, and costing
  • Cross-dock, VAS, kitting, and customer-specific workflows
  • Integration with ERP, TMS, customer portals, and reporting

CPG / FMCG Warehousing

Trangile supports high-volume CPG warehouses with Infor WMS processes for batch control, quality status, expiry management, traceability, and paperless execution.

  • Batch, lot, expiry, FEFO, and QC-led movement
  • SSCC, pallet, case, and traceability compliance
  • High-throughput inbound, replenishment, and dispatch

Fashion & Apparel

Trangile enables fashion and apparel businesses to run Infor WMS around style, size, color, seasonality, pack handling, store replenishment, and ecommerce complexity.

  • Style, size, color, article, and seasonal inventory control
  • Piece-level scanning and complex pack scenarios
  • Returns, store replenishment, and ecommerce fulfillment

Food & Beverages Distribution

For food and beverages warehouses, Trangile shapes Infor WMS around expiry discipline, recall readiness, quality controls, and fast-moving SKU replenishment.

  • FEFO, batch, lot, expiry, and quality status control
  • Temperature-sensitive handling and replenishment visibility
  • Traceability, recall readiness, and DC throughput optimization

Manufacturing & Distribution

Trangile helps manufacturers and distributors use Infor WMS to control parts, finished goods, serials, batches, production support flows, and ERP-led warehouse execution.

  • Serial, batch, FIFO/FEFO, and quality-status inventory control
  • Multi-entity warehouse execution and replenishment logic
  • ERP-led visibility across inbound, storage, picking, and dispatch

Automobile & Auto Components

Trangile configures Infor WMS for automotive warehouses managing spares, kits, supplier inventory, line-side replenishment, and production support requirements.

  • Parts, spares, kits, and component-level visibility
  • Supplier, serial, batch, and inspection status tracking
  • ERP, supplier, transport, and production integration

Aerospace & Precision Manufacturing

Trangile supports precision manufacturing and aerospace warehouse scenarios where auditability, traceability, controlled movement, and compliance-driven workflows are critical.

  • High-value parts traceability and controlled inventory movement
  • Lot, serial, compliance, and inspection status management
  • Integration with ERP, quality, maintenance, and supplier systems
